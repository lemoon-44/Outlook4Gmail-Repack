# Outlook4Gmail

## Overview

Outlook4Gmail is a synchronization tool designed to integrate Google Contacts and Google Calendar with Microsoft Outlook. It facilitates seamless data exchange between these platforms, allowing users to keep their contacts and calendar events consistent across both services. The project targets Windows users who require efficient management of their schedules and contacts within Outlook while maintaining synchronization with their Google accounts.

## Key Features

**Two-way synchronization of Google Contacts and Microsoft Outlook contacts**  
**Synchronization of Google Calendar events with Outlook calendar**  
**Scheduled synchronization options for automated updates**  
**Conflict detection and resolution during data synchronization**  
**Support for multiple Google accounts within Outlook**  
**Selective synchronization of calendars and contact groups**  
**Customizable synchronization intervals and settings**  
**User-friendly interface integrated into Outlook ribbon**  

## Why Outlook4Gmail?

Outlook4Gmail offers a clear and reliable method for maintaining consistent contact and calendar data between Google and Outlook environments. The software emphasizes usability by integrating directly into the Outlook interface, reducing the need for manual data management. Its focus on stable synchronization processes helps minimize discrepancies and data conflicts, making it a practical choice for users managing information across these platforms.

## Benefits

Synchronization reduces manual data entry and the risk of outdated information.  
Maintaining consistent contacts and calendar events streamlines personal and professional scheduling.  
Automation of synchronization tasks saves time and reduces errors.  
Integration with Outlook ensures that users do not need to switch between multiple applications.  

## Compatibility

This repository is developed specifically for Windows operating systems. It is optimized to provide stable performance and efficient operation within the Windows environment, leveraging Outlook native APIs for integration.
